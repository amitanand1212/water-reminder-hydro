Privacy Policy

App: Hydro (water reminder : Hydro)

Effective date: 24 June 2026

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Hydro ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. The App is provided by water reminder : Hydro. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

In short: Hydro has no accounts, servers, or databases. Everything you enter stays on your own device. We never receive or sell your data. The only third party that processes any data is Google AdMob, which we use to display ads.

1. Summary

Hydro is a water-reminder app. We do not have any accounts, servers, or databases. All of the information you enter stays on your own device. We never see it, never receive it, and never sell it. The only third party that processes any data is Google AdMob, which we use to display ads.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information stored only on your device

When you use the App, the following information is saved locally on your device (using your device's app storage) so the App can work. This data is not transmitted to us or to any server:

  • Your name (as entered during setup)
  • Your weight and weight unit (kg or lb)
  • Your daily hydration goal
  • Your water intake logs (amounts and timestamps)
  • Your streaks, XP, levels, and earned badges
  • Your app settings (units, reminders configuration, theme)

This information never leaves your device except if you manually back it up through your own device/OS backup features, which are outside our control.

2.2 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in.
  • We do not collect your real name, email, or phone number through the App.
  • We do not record audio or access your microphone. The App only plays a short sound effect; it never records.
  • We do not access your location, contacts, photos, or camera.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google may collect and process certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, and ad-interaction data, in accordance with Google's own policies. The App is configured to request non-personalized ads.

You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google Advertising policies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Notifications

The App sends local reminder notifications (for example, "Time to drink water!") based on the schedule you choose. These notifications are generated on your device. We do not operate a push-notification server, and notification content is not sent to us. You can turn reminders off at any time inside the App or in your device's system settings.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to show you hydration reminders.
  • Exact alarms / Schedule exact alarm / Run at startup — so that scheduled reminders fire reliably and continue after your device restarts.

Note: The App's package may declare an audio-related permission, but the App does not record audio — it only plays a short sound effect. No audio data is ever captured, stored, or transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising served through the App is configured as non-personalized. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can assist.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored only on your device, you are in full control of it. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Using the reset/clear option inside the App, or
  • Uninstalling the App, which permanently removes all locally stored data.

We do not retain any copy of your data because we never receive it.

8. Data Security

Your information is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's own security mechanisms. Since we do not transmit or store your data on external servers, there is no server-side data for us to secure or expose.

9. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally and controlled by you, you can access, modify, or delete it directly within the App at any time. If you have questions about your rights, contact us using the details below.
